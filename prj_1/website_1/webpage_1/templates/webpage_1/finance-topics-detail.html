National Stock Exchange (NSE)

Founded: 1992, and it quickly became one of the largest exchanges in India.

Location: Mumbai, India.

Index:The NSE's benchmark index is the Nifty 50, consisting of 50 major stocks representing key sectors of the Indian economy.

Features: NSE was the first exchange in India to provide a fully automated screen-based electronic trading system. It offers trading in various segments, including equity, derivatives, currency derivatives, and debt.

Both BSE and NSE are regulated by the Securities and Exchange Board of India (SEBI), which ensures that the markets operate transparently and efficiently.
